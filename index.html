History

Original email from James on October 8, 2011:

OK, here's the deal, CK and Jenny were over and we were discussing a "new to most" restaurant club.

Here are the guidelines:

A restaurant will be chosen that most of us haven't been to.

Dinners will take place the last Tuesday of the month, time TBD but usually around 7:00 pm.

Let the Grand Poo-bah (GPB) (the person who chose the restaurant of the month) know if you will be attending the dinner by time mentioned in the e-mail from the GPB.

At the dinner, the GPB will pass the torch to the person who will serve as the next GPB.

Everyone won't be able to make every dinner, and that's OK, we won't hold it against you.

Here are the details for the first dinner:

October 25th 7:00 pm

Dewey's Pizza in Grandview

Grand Poo-bah is Dr. Megan Norris, RSVP to her by Monday, October 24th @ 11:59 pm EST

We appreciate your participation, if you wish to unsubscribe, have the balls to hit REPLY ALL and explain your reasoning, and then go SCREW YOURSELF.:P

Thank you for your time.
